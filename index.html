Frequently Asked Questions

How do I verify my carbon credits?

Our admin team reviews all listings for authenticity. Verification typically takes 2-3 business days.

What payment methods are accepted?

We support online payments via UPI, bank transfer, and offline payments for verified users.

How are prices determined?

Prices are set by farmers based on market rates, farming practices, and verification standards.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect all user data.
